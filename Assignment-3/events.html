Events & Competitions

Explore all categories and pick what you’ll compete in. Use the lists below for formats, team sizes, and quick rules.

Tech

  1. NovaHack 24 — Hackathon

    • Format: 24‑hour on‑site hackathon
    • Team size: 2–4
    • Theme: Campus Sustainability
    • Fee: $15 per team

    Build prototypes that make campus life greener. Shortlisted teams demo to a panel of industry judges.

  2. CodeSprint — Competitive Programming

    • Format: 2‑hour contest (ICPC style)
    • Team size: 1–3
    • Languages: C/C++/Java/Python
    • Fee: $5 per participant

    Solve algorithmic problems under pressure. Tie‑break by total time.

  3. RoboLine — Line Follower Robotics

    • Format: Track race with obstacles
    • Team size: 2–5
    • Track: Provided on‑site
    • Fee: $10 per team

    Bring your bot and blaze through the course. Sensors and controllers are your call.

  4. PixelCraft — UI/UX Design

    • Format: 3‑hour design challenge
    • Team size: Solo
    • Tooling: Your choice (bring laptop)
    • Fee: $5

    Design a mobile app for students with accessibility at its core. Present your flows and mockups.

Cultural

  • Short Film Showcase

    • Format: 5–8 min film
    • Team size: Any
    • Theme: Campus Stories
    • Fee: $5 per film

  • FrameIt — Photography

    • Format: On‑campus photo walk + submission
    • Team size: Solo
    • Theme: Patterns & People
    • Fee: $3

Sports
