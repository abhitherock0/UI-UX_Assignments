FAQs

Who can participate?

Students from any accredited college can join. Bring a valid student ID to collect your badge.

How do I pay the registration fee?

Choose UPI, card, or cash on the registration form. Payment instructions follow in your confirmation email.

Can I register for multiple events?

Yes — just tick all events you want on the form. Watch for timing clashes in the schedule.

Is on‑spot registration allowed?

Limited on‑spot entries are available for select events on a first‑come basis.

Where can I see last year’s photos?

Head to the Previous Year Gallery (2024).

About NovaFest

Nova College Fest is a student-run celebration of innovation, culture, and sport. Join us for three days of competitions, showcases, and community.
